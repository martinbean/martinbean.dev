Web Development Projects

Below you’ll find a sample of some web development projects I’ve been lucky enough to work on as a freelance web developer. If you would like more details on the work I’ve done or would like to discuss your requirements, then do get in touch.

BAMMA

BAMMA is Europe’s premier mixed martial arts promotion, with live events broadcasts on Channel 5 and sister channel 5✪ in the UK. BAMMA promotes the cream of upcoming British talent alongside international stars.

In mid-2013, BAMMA came to me looking to revamp their web presence. I built a bespoke website framework and content management system with CakePHP, and heavily customised Zurb Foundation for the front-end design.

Just Dance® 2014

Ubisoft Reflections got in touch with me in the summer of 2013, looking for someone to develop their HTML5 and JavaScript framework for the Xbox SmartGlass version of Just Dance® 2014.

Working with gameplay programmers, I transformed Reflection’s prototype into an event-driven framework communicating with the Xbox 360 and Xbox One console versions of the title.

ScS Sofas

During my time at ScS I built the “Sleep Centre” section of the website and integrated it with their bespoke shopping cart. I also developed various tools as part of this process, including mass import tools for various manufacturers’ CSV files.

I also integrated new templates into the website and took the time to start re-factoring the front-end HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and crating a style guide.

Whilst at ScS, I also introduced industry-standard practices such as version control with Git, and automated code deployment in place of FTP.

Lostbox

Lostbox is a new lost and found website that uses the power of social media to extend their reach and get people reunited with their missing items as quickly as possible.

I was commissioned to design and develop the website. The front-end was built with Twitter Bootstrap based on the logo provided by the client, and the website was built using the CakePHP framework and includes a custom-built administration area to manage users, items, categories and adverts.

Bdaily

Working as part of a team of three, we re-factored the entire Bdaily website, making use of Symfony components and Amazon Web Services along the way. With a solid foundation in place, we then began developing new features for the website.

One of my main tasks during my time working on the Bdaily website was the creation of a new advertisements system that allowed members to specify and purchase advert placements on the site. Purchases were then handled by the GoCardless payment gateway.

JW Wood Estate Agents

JW Wood is one the North East’s largest estate agents, with six offices in the region.

Working alongside another agency, I created a bespoke ExpressionEngine module that parsed RightMove data feeds and ensured JW Wood’s inventory of properties was kept up to date on their website.
